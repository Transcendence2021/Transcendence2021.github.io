Gaming

“Whoever said the pen is mightier than the sword obviously never encountered automatic weapons” – Douglas MacArthur

Gamers, do you possess the mettle required to persevere and survive through the slimmest odds to come out on top? Choose from a wide spectrum of games ranging from old school chess to competitive FPS games like VALORANT and CS:GO. This is your chance to prove your worth and display the best skills that you have in your arsenal. In the present circumstances, a lot of like-minded people in our age group have turned to gaming as solace from the monotony of life. Transcendence seeks to bring out the best frags, aces, goals, and checkmates from the gamers all around the world.




Guidelines


CSGO (PC)

  • Every participant requires a FaceIT account, the client and anticheat installed. Instructions to do so will be intimated to participants as they register.(NOTE: FaceIt requires to be linked to a Steam account which has CS:GO played in it's library for AT LEAST 25 hours; the anti-cheat can only be installed on Windows 8.1 and 10. Participants CANNOT use previous versions or MacOS.)
  • Competitive-Defusal (5v5)
  • Map to be decided by pick and ban system
  • Direct Knockouts. BO3 Finals.
  • Event is capped at 16 teams.

Valorant (PC)

  • Competitive- Defusal (5v5)
  • Map to be decided by pick and ban system.
  • Direct Knockouts. BO3 Finals.
  • Event is capped at 32 teams.

Rocket League

  • Standard (3v3)
  • BO3 for group stages. Finals BO5.
  • All platforms are welcome. Participants must have an Epic Games ID linked to their platform of choice.

Chess

  • Every participant must have a chess.com account
  • Google Chrome is the preferred browser
  • Solo event
  • Round 1-Swiss tournament
    Clock: 3+2
    Top 8 per bracket qualify for round 2
  • Round 2- Direct knockout
    Clock:10+5
  • Finals BO3
    Clock:10+5

Call of Duty Mobile

  • Quads
  • Initial gamemode: Battle Royale
    Point system - 3 points per kill
            - 5 points for first kill
            - 10 points for 1st place, 9 points for 2nd place….. 1 point for 10th place
    Top 4 teams qualify to the next round
  • Knockout gamemode: Hardpoint
  • Finals: Search and Destroy
  • No external controllers or emulators are allowed. Non-compliance may lead to disqualification.


General Guidelines

  • If any evidence suggests teaming/ team-sabotage, it is at the discretion of the event-heads to disqualify the team/participant.
  • All communication to teams will be done via discord.
  • Participation is encouraged only in the South East Asia region, to prevent issues relating to latency and packet loss.
  • Participants must ensure that their game is updated to the latest versions well before the assigned time.
