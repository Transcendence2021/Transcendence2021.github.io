Registrations

Instructions


  • Participant names, a contact number per individual, a valid email, and respective IDs must be submitted during registration.
  • Gaming participants must also submit their usernames/gamertag.
  • When representing a school, teacher coordinator and student coordinator details will also have to be submitted.
  • To be applicable for the All Event combo you must confirm you are taking part in all non-gaming events in the registration form.
  • A payment link will be emailed to the email provided. Once payment is received, an invite to a WhatsApp/Telegram group will be sent to the respective email.
